❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my subscription? ▾
You can cancel your subscription anytime from Settings → Account → Cancel Subscription. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
Why is my profile photo not showing? ▾
Go to Settings and re-upload your profile photo. Make sure it's a JPG or PNG under 5MB. If the issue persists, submit a support ticket above.
How does AI pattern detection work? ▾
Our AI analyzes your daily check-ins over time to detect unusual patterns. It compares your current readings against your personal baseline and flags anything concerning.
My medical report analysis seems wrong — what should I do? ▾
AI analysis is for educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified doctor for medical advice. If you believe there's a technical error, submit a ticket with the report details.
How do I add family members? ▾
Go to the Family page and enter the email address of the person you want to add. They need to have a HealthGuardian account. If they don't, we'll send them an invite email.
Is my health data secure? ▾
Yes! All your health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties. You can delete all your data anytime from Settings → Danger Zone.
