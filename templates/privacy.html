Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: 1 January 2026 | Effective date: 1 January 2026

🛡️ Short version: We collect only what we need to provide our service. We never sell your health data. You can delete your data at any time. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Who We Are

HealthGuardian ("we", "our", "us") is a health monitoring platform that helps individuals track their daily health and detect patterns early using artificial intelligence. Our platform is accessible at healthguardian.app.

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: privacy@healthguardian.app

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name and email address (required to create an account)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your actual password)
  • Optional profile details: age, gender, profile photo

Health Data

  • Daily check-in data: energy levels, sleep quality, mood, pain levels, appetite, stress
  • Optional notes you add to check-ins
  • Symptom checker inputs and AI responses
  • AI chat conversation history

Technical Data

  • IP address and browser type (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Usage analytics (pages visited, features used — anonymised)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store card details)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the HealthGuardian service
  • To run AI pattern analysis on your health data
  • To send you health alerts and notifications you've requested
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

We never use your health data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the service you've signed up for
  • Consent: For health data processing, which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legitimate interests: Security, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
  • Cloud hosting providers — Secure data storage
  • AI service providers — To power our AI features (data is anonymised where possible)

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal or health data to any third party.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for stored data
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to certain types of processing
  • Restriction — Request we limit how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@healthguardian.app or use the account deletion option in Settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal and health data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised aggregate data may be retained for service improvement.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and protect your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

10. Children's Privacy

HealthGuardian is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately at privacy@healthguardian.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or in-app notification. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights: privacy@healthguardian.app

Or write to us via our Contact page.
