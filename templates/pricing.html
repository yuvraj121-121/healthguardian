Is my health data safe? ▾
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and never shared. We are fully GDPR compliant and you can delete your data anytime.
How does AI pattern detection work? ▾
Our AI analyzes your daily check-ins over time to detect patterns indicating early health issues. It learns from your personal baseline and alerts you when something unusual is detected.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▾
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.
