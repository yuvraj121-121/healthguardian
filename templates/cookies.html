Cookie Policy 🍪

Last updated: 1 January 2026

🍪 Short version: We only use essential cookies to keep you logged in and keep the site secure. We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, like whether you're logged in.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
session Keeps you logged in securely Essential Until you log out
remember_token Remembers your login across browser sessions Essential 30 days
csrf_token Security — prevents cross-site request forgery attacks Essential Session

Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • ❌ Advertising or marketing cookies
  • ❌ Third-party tracking cookies
  • ❌ Analytics cookies that identify you personally
  • ❌ Social media cookies

Managing Cookies

Since we only use essential cookies, disabling them will prevent the site from working correctly — you won't be able to log in. You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
