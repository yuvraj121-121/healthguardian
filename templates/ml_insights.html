💡 Wellness Tips
😴
Sleep Quality
Try to maintain a consistent sleep schedule. Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.
🧘
Stress Management
Practice deep breathing or meditation for 5-10 minutes daily. Even short sessions can significantly reduce stress levels.
🏃
Physical Activity
Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily. Walking, cycling, or swimming are excellent low-impact options.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration improves energy levels, mood, and cognitive function.
🥗
Balanced Diet
Include fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains in your meals. Avoid processed foods and excessive sugar.
📱
Digital Detox
Reduce screen time before bed. Blue light from devices can disrupt sleep patterns and increase stress levels.
